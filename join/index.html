1. Sign up on beINvolved

beINvolved is IU's central hub for all student-run organizations on campus. By joining us there, you will be registered on the club's official roster.

Join the CS Club on beINvolved

2. Subscribe to our mailing list

The CS Club mailing list is where all important announcements go first. Make sure you're subscribed to get vital information. To do so, send an empty email to csclub-l-subscribe@list.indiana.edu, or go to the csclub-l list page and click "Subscribe" on the left.

3. Fill out the New Member form

Fill out the New Member Information form to the right to tell us a bit about yourself. This helps us get an idea of who's in the club so that we can tailor our activities to your interests.

4. Come to an event!


The club meets every week. Check out our event schedule to see what's going on this semester. You can also see our next event to the left.

Want to chat with other members? Join our GroupMe!
